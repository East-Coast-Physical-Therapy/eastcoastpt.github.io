Meet Our Doctors

HEADSHOT: DR. ETHAN ROSEN

Dr. Ethan Rosen, PT, DPT, CSCS

Ethan is a Doctor of Physical Therapy and a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) dedicated to a holistic, movement-first approach to health. A graduate of Tufts University, Ethan’s clinical expertise is backed by a Bachelor’s in Sports and Movement Science and an Associate degree in Dietetics and Clinical Nutrition Services, allowing him to integrate rehabilitative medicine with foundational wellness.

With over seven years of experience as a personal trainer and specialized certifications in Corrective Exercise (CES) and Senior Fitness, Ethan bridges the gap between injury recovery and high-level performance. He is a lifelong athlete and avid runner who believes that daily movement is the cornerstone of longevity. Whether working with athletes or those looking to move pain-free, Ethan focuses on optimizing mechanics and empowering his patients through a deep, evidence-based understanding of the human body.

HEADSHOT: DR. MIA LEMIEUX

Dr. Mia Lemieux, PT, DPT

Mia is a doctor of physical therapy whose clinical practice is deeply rooted in a lifetime of movement and artistic expression. A dancer since the age of three and a dance instructor for over seven years, Mia pairs an athlete's intuition with advanced clinical training. She earned her Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Tufts University and a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from The University of Massachusetts Amherst, establishing a rigorous foundation in human mechanics. As a Herman & Wallace Level 1 Pelvic Health Clinician and a Certified Autism Exercise Specialist, Mia offers a specialized, inclusive approach to rehabilitation that considers the unique needs of every patient.

Mia’s professional focus is driven by a profound personal mission: her passion for treating pelvic pain stems from her own lived experience. This personal journey allows her to provide a rare level of empathy and advocacy for those navigating pelvic floor dysfunction. By combining her background in dance medicine with evidence-based pelvic health interventions, Mia provides a holistic, compassionate environment where patients feel truly heard. She is dedicated to helping individuals move beyond pain and return to the activities they love with confidence and ease.
